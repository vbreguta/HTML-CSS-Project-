Welcome to Kitty Heaven Cafe

Do you like cats and coffee? If yes, this is the best place where you can be. Kitty heaven is a cafe located in the suburbs of Chicago. It is in the middle of a park, surrounded by trees, lakes, and flowers. Once you enter our cafe you will be greeted by cats who wait to be pet and loved.
We are a small family business who's goal is to save as many cats as posible, giving them a life as in heaven. We give you the chance to fall in love with our foster cats and give them a forever home. Even if you don't feel ready to adopt a cat, you have the chance to make them feel your love while relaxing and drinking coffee.

Things you can do at Kitty Heaven:

  • Spend time with cats
  • Drik Coffee
  • Have a small snack, backery
  • Study
  • Read books from our library
